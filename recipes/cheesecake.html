Afuega'l Pitu Cheesecake

Description

An Afuega'l Pitu Cheesecake is a traditional dessert of Asturian cuisine which is made from Afuega'l Pitu Cheese and other ingredients such as Condensed milk, Fresh milk, Sugar and Eggs.

Ingredients

For 6/8 diners:

  • 200 gr Afuega'l Pitu white cheese.
  • 25 gr Afuega'l Pitu red cheese.
  • 400 ml condensed milk.
  • 600 ml liquid cream.
  • 200 ml fresh milk.
  • 125 gr sugar.
  • 3 eggs.
  • Jam.
  • Salt.

Steps

  1. Whisk the cheeses, condensed milk, cream, fresh milk, sugar, eggs and salt in a bowl.
  2. Put the result in a cake tin and bake it at 165ÂºC for 45 minutes.
  3. Leave to cool and add jam on top to taste.
