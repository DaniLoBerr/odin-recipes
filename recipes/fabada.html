Asturian Fabada

Description

An Asturian Fabada is a traditional dish of Asturian cuisine which is made with Asturian Faba beans (in Asturian, "Fabes") and pork and sausages such as Asturian Chorizo and Asturian Black Pudding (in Asturian, "Compango").

Ingredients

For 4 diners:

  • 1/2 kg of Fabes de la Granja (beans from the farm).
  • 2 Chorizo sausages.
  • 2 black puddings.
  • A piece of pork shoulder.
  • A piece of bacon.
  • A piece of meat (pig's trotter, ear, rib...).

Steps

  1. Soak the beans in water the night before.
  2. To start cooking them, drain them, change the water until they are completely covered.
  3. Add the compango (the chorizo, black pudding, pork shoulder, bacon and meat) and leave to cook until the fabes are tender.
  4. During cooking, remove the foam as they are cooking.
  5. If the water is used up, add more, always cold. Add salt to taste.
  6. At the end, you can add some garlic and paprika.
