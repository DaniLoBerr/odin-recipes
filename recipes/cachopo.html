Asturian Cachopo

Description

A Cachopo is a traditional dish of Asturian cuisine which is made from two large fillets of beef between which it is stuffed with Iberian ham and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 fillets of IGP Asturian Beef.
  • 4 thin slices of Asturian Cheese.
  • 4 thin slices of Iberian Ham.
  • 100 gr white flour.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 200g breadcrumbs.
  • Salt.
  • Pepper.
  • Olive Oil.
